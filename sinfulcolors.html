Project for SinfulColors (Patent Pending)

August 1, 2014 - Febuary 1, 2014

An Android app that utilizes a consumer's phone camera to take a picture of an outfit. The app allows the user to select a color from the picture. After the user selects the color they wish to match, the app uses the Color Lab Space algorithm to suggest appropriate nail polishes.

Uses Android's camera API, SQLite, and the Color Lab Space algorithm.

A patent has been filed by SinfulColors, naming Parth Mehrotra as the inventor

You can check out the app on the Play Store
